Chasing the Northern Lights in Iceland

Join Lucas Snow on an unforgettable journey through the rugged landscapes of Iceland, where you'll chase the elusive Northern Lights across the dark, starry sky. This adventure starts in Reykjavik, where you'll explore the famous Golden Circle—seeing the Geysir hot springs, Gullfoss waterfall, and the Þingvellir National Park. Then, you'll venture into the wilderness, staying in cozy lodges where the night sky is the main attraction. The highlight is, of course, the stunning Aurora Borealis, an ethereal light show that paints the sky with vibrant greens, purples, and reds.

Highlights:

  • Northern Lights hunting from scenic viewpoints
  • Snowmobile tours on glaciers
  • Geothermal hot springs to warm up after a day of exploration

Dog Sledding in the Canadian Rockies

Embark on a thrilling dog sledding adventure through the snow-covered trails of the Canadian Rockies. Under Lucas Snow's guidance, you'll learn the basics of mushing, before setting off through pristine, frozen landscapes with your team of energetic sled dogs. Along the way, you'll encounter towering mountain peaks, frozen lakes, and dense pine forests, all blanketed in fresh snow. After a day of mushing, you'll retreat to a cozy lodge, where you can enjoy hot chocolate by the fire and swap stories with fellow adventurers.

Highlights:

  • Dog sledding across vast frozen terrains
  • Overnight stays in remote, scenic cabins
  • A chance to connect with nature and the dog teams

Snowshoeing in Lapland

Experience the winter wonderland of Lapland, where snowshoeing takes you through tranquil forests and across frozen lakes, offering unparalleled views of the wintry landscape. With Lucas Snow's expert guidance, you'll learn about the indigenous Sami culture, visit a reindeer farm, and enjoy traditional Lappish cuisine. The tour culminates with a visit to the Santa Claus Village, where you can cross the Arctic Circle and send postcards from the "official" home of Santa.

Highlights:

  • Snowshoeing and cross-country skiing through untouched forests
  • Meeting reindeer and learning about Sami traditions
  • A night in an ice hotel with an ice bar and ice sculptures

Hiking the Swiss Alps in Winter

For those who prefer a more active winter escape, Lucas Snow offers a winter hiking adventure in the Swiss Alps. This immersive experience combines stunning scenery with physical challenge as you hike through snow-covered valleys and up mountain slopes, taking in panoramic views of the majestic Alps. Each night, you'll stay in charming alpine villages, sampling Swiss delicacies and unwinding in mountain lodges, ready to tackle another breathtaking trail the next day.

Highlights:

  • Guided winter hikes through snow-dusted meadows
  • Alpine village exploration with fondue dinners
  • Cable car rides to some of the highest peaks in Europe

Winter Wildlife Safari in Norway

Join Lucas Snow for a wildlife safari in Norway's Arctic Circle, where you'll have the chance to witness polar bears, Arctic foxes, and reindeer in their natural winter habitats. The tour includes a boat trip through the icy waters of the Arctic Ocean, where you might spot humpback whales and orcas. Spend the evenings in cozy cabins, enjoying the peace of the Norwegian wilderness under the northern sky.

Highlights:

  • Wildlife watching in Norway's frozen fjords
  • A boat trip through Arctic waters to see marine life
  • Spending nights in traditional Norwegian cabins, surrounded by nature
